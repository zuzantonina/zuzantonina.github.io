ABOUT

Zuzanna Antonina Wieczorek, born in 2005 is a Polish artist. Based in The Netherlands, she is pursuing BA degree in photography with her focus also on Graphic Design. Gallerytalk team. After assisting at Kunsthalle Basel in Switzerland, she has been working for Sfeir-Semler Gallery, focusing on digital communication and social media. During the COVID-19 pandemic, she contributed to the non-profit project Kunstnothilfe , a solidarity fund to support art and culture professionals in need. In collaboration with institutions such as E-Werk Freiburg and Südufer, she co-curated several interdisciplinary events, festivals and exhibitions, for instance New Old Black Forest. While living in the Netherlands, she assisted in implementing the Corporate Identity for the young art fair UNFAIR Amsterdam. As a lecturer at Macromedia, she led the pre-studies course and held several lectures and workshops on the topics of design ethics, web accessibility, content moderation and censorship.
